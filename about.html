About our Church

Hope Assembly of God was founded over seventy years ago in Colfax, Iowa. We are a part of the Assemblies of God General Council worldwide fellowship of churches. We are an evangelical, Pentecostal fellowship with a strong missions focus both at home and abroad reaching out to people all over the world with the good news of Jesus Christ.

We do several community outreaches a year. We take part in the Jasper County Fair parade, Halloween Walk, Trunk or Treat/Candy Carnival, Vacation Bible School, Bikes and Burger day, and more.

We have members on the Colfax Food Pantry Board and other benevolence ministries are available.

We feature live music in a contemporary worship service, as well as bible-based preaching and teaching for all ages on Sunday mornings and Wednesday nights. Part of the services we offer are basic pre and post marital counseling and family counseling as well as other helpful resources.





About our Pastor

Rev. Paul Avery has been in active ministry for about 40 years. Credentialed in the North Texas District in 1989, Pastor Paul has been in pastoral ministry for 35 years in a variety of positions. He has worked in Children's ministry, Youth ministry, Evangelical outreach, visitation, education, as well as Associate and Lead Pastor positions. Paul is currently on the Jasper County Sheriff's Department D.A.C.S. Critical Incident Stress Management team working with first responders' debriefings. His wife, Teresa, works with the Women's Bible study, Children's Church, nursery, and is one of the main team event organizers for community outreach. Married in 1985, they have worked together in the Lord's service ever since.





About Assemblies of God

Founded in 1914, Assemblies of God is one of the world's largest Pentecostal denomination with close to 13,000 churches in the U.S. and nearly 3 million members and adherents. Assemblies of God focuses on the 'Four Core Beliefs': Salvation, Baptism in the Holy Spirit, Divine Healing, and the Second Coming of Christ.
